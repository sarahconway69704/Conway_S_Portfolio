It all started when Sarah fought her way out of her mother’s womb. She arrived very late. Fashionably late. “This is the coolest baby I’ve ever seen!” cried the Doctor.

Let’s skip ahead.

At 18, Sarah left her beloved hometown (or homecity?) of Toronto, Ontario to pursue higher education. She didn’t know what she wanted to do with her life. All she knew was that she loved art and spent an absurd amount of time on her computer.

Sarah found a program that fit her two loves (addictions). It would allow her to go to Western University AND Fanshawe College. She would deepen her critical thinking of media theory at Western, and learn practical interactive media design skills at Fanshawe.

4 years later, she got really good at things.
